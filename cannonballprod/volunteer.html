VOLUNTEER

Get valuable production experience in a fun, welcoming environment by volunteering at one of our events!


Our volunteers help with a range of important projects, including pouring beer, managing the entry gates, checking tickets, arranging game booths, selling merchandise, assisting restaurants, applying temporary tattoos and more.


Every volunteer receives a branded t-shirt and a souvenir cup at the end of their shift. They also help a good cause and earn community service hours; a portion of the proceeds from our events alwawys go to charity.
