OUR STORY

Cannonball Productions is a New York City-based, M/WBE-certified event production company dedicated to creating big, bold, one-of-a-kind experiences. Our national food and beverage festivals--from the Bacon and Beer Classic to Lions Tigers and Brews--draw sold-out crowds across the country while our Chicago-based adventures--from winery tours to water tubing--have become a Midwest tradition.


In addition to our passion for events and new experiences, we bring together a diverse mix of perspectives and expertise, a commitment to hard work and integrity, and many years of experiences.
